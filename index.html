Built for how you train

From your first rep to your personal record — GymTracker covers every aspect of your training.

Training Programs

Create structured programs with custom exercises, sets, reps, and rest periods. Import from PDF or text automatically.

Live Workout Tracking

Log sets in real time with automatic rest timers. Every rep recorded, every weight saved.

Strength Analytics

Volume charts, muscle distribution, progression curves, and personal records — tracked over weeks and months.

Apple Health

Syncs workouts, heart rate, calories, and body weight bidirectionally with Apple Health.

Apple Watch

Start and control workouts from your wrist. Live Activities display sets, reps, and rest timers in real time.

Cloud Sync

Your data syncs across all devices. Works offline and reconciles automatically when back online.

Program Import

Import workout plans from PDFs, images, or plain text. The app parses exercises and builds the program for you.

Offline First

No connection required to train. All data is stored locally and synced when connectivity returns.

Designed for iPhone

Dark-first interface with full light mode support. Every screen built for clarity and speed.
