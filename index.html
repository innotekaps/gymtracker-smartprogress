Privacy Policy – GymTracker

Last updated: April 3, 2026

GymTracker ("we", "our", "the app") is developed by INNOVET (innovetservizi@gmail.com). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use the GymTracker mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Data

When you sign in with Google or create an account, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture to identify your account and sync your data across devices.

Fitness & Health Data (HealthKit)

With your explicit permission, GymTracker reads and writes the following data from/to Apple Health:

  • Workout sessions (type, duration, calories burned)
  • Heart rate measurements
  • Body weight
  • Active energy burned

This data is used solely to display your fitness statistics and calculate performance metrics within the app. Health data is never shared with third parties or used for advertising purposes.

Motion & Activity Data

GymTracker may access motion sensors to detect physical activity during workouts. This data is processed on-device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Workout & Training Data

We store your training schedules, exercise logs, personal records, and progress history in our secure cloud database (Firebase / Firestore) to enable cross-device sync and backup.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide, personalise, and improve the GymTracker experience
  • To sync your workouts and progress across your devices
  • To calculate fitness statistics and performance trends
  • To send optional workout reminders (only if you enable notifications)

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore and Authentication), which complies with industry-standard security practices. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase – authentication, cloud database: Firebase Privacy
  • Google Sign-In – social login: Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple HealthKit – health data integration (data stays on-device / iCloud)

5. Data Retention & Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the app settings (Settings → Account → Delete Account). Upon deletion, all your personal data and workout history will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

6. Children's Privacy

GymTracker is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at innovetservizi@gmail.com.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
📧 innovetservizi@gmail.com

Informativa sulla Privacy – GymTracker

Ultimo aggiornamento: 3 aprile 2026

GymTracker è sviluppata da INNOVET (innovetservizi@gmail.com). Questa informativa spiega come raccogliamo, utilizziamo e proteggiamo i tuoi dati.

1. Dati raccolti

Dati account

Accedendo con Google raccogliamo nome, email e foto profilo per identificare il tuo account e sincronizzare i dati tra i dispositivi.

Dati sanitari (HealthKit)

Con il tuo esplicito consenso, GymTracker legge e scrive su Apple Salute:

  • Sessioni di allenamento (tipo, durata, calorie)
  • Frequenza cardiaca
  • Peso corporeo
  • Energia attiva bruciata

Questi dati vengono usati esclusivamente per mostrare statistiche e calcolare le performance. Non vengono condivisi con terze parti né usati per pubblicità.

Dati di movimento

GymTracker può accedere ai sensori di movimento per rilevare l'attività fisica. Questi dati sono elaborati sul dispositivo e non trasmessi ai nostri server.

Allenamenti e progressi

Le tue schede, i log degli esercizi e i record personali vengono salvati sul nostro database sicuro (Firebase / Firestore) per la sincronizzazione e il backup.

2. Come usiamo i tuoi dati

  • Per fornire e migliorare l'esperienza GymTracker
  • Per sincronizzare i tuoi allenamenti tra dispositivi
  • Per calcolare statistiche e progressi
  • Per inviare promemoria (solo se abiliti le notifiche)

3. Conservazione ed eliminazione

Puoi eliminare il tuo account e tutti i dati associati dalle impostazioni dell'app (Impostazioni → Account → Elimina account). I dati verranno rimossi definitivamente entro 30 giorni.

4. Servizi di terze parti

  • Google Firebase – autenticazione e database cloud
  • Google Sign-In – accesso social
  • Apple HealthKit – integrazione dati salute
